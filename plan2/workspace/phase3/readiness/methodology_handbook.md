# Phase 3 — Pre-Selection Sunday Readiness: Methodology Handbook

## Purpose
Define repeatable decision policy before bracket reveal to prevent narrative-driven overrides.

## Core rules
1. **Probability-first picks**: every pick maps to model probability and uncertainty tier.
2. **Constrained upset budget**: maximum upset allocations per round:
   - Round of 64: 6–9
   - Round of 32: 3–5
   - Sweet 16+: selective only where edge >= 6 pts probability.
3. **No single-source overrides**: qualitative narratives cannot overturn quantitative consensus alone.

## Round weighting policy
- Early rounds prioritize matchup-specific edges and rotation depth stability.
- Late rounds increase weight on top-end efficiency ceiling and guard creation resilience.

## Evidence hierarchy
1. Verified injury/availability updates.
2. Ensemble probability + calibration interval.
3. Market movement confirmation.
4. Matchup dossier qualitative notes.

## Governance
- All non-chalk picks require one-line rationale and evidence tags.
- Manual override allowed only with two-of-three agreement (model, market, injury signal).
- Lock cutoff: no discretionary changes after final checkpoint.
